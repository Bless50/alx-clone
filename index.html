Introduction To Software Engineering

Learn the fundamentals of software engineering

About the course

Dive into the exciting world of software engineering with us! Our program provides a clear and straightforward introduction to the fundamentals of coding.

We've designed it to be easily understandable, no matter your background or experience level. It's like piecing together a puzzle, bit by bit, each piece leading you closer to becoming a software engineer.

Who will benefit

You are an absolute newbie with zero coding background or you have limited coding knowledge

You have limited time availability and want something that fits well in your busy schedule

You have a strong desire to learn the basics of software engineering

Program Structure

Project-Based Learning

Practical tasks and projects help you apply your skills to build fully functional web applications.

Flexible Learning Experience

Learn at a flexible pace with support and feedback from mentors and peers.

Comprehensive Curriculum

Build good fundamentals in all the essential tools and technologies to develop a full web application.

Weekly Schedule

  • Projects for the week released on Saturdays; due by Friday
  • Live learning sessions on Tuesdays and Fridays
  • Peer learning on Wednesdays for collaboration, discussion, and insights sharing

Progressing Through The
Program

  • Weekly compulsory evaluation quizzes
  • End of section mega quizzes
  • Minimum of one project per week
  • Two-week capstone project to be done individually

Graduation Criteria

To graduate, you need to:

  • Submit all projects and pass all quizzes with an average score of 60% or higher
  • Participate in, present, and pass an assessment for the capstone project

Frequently Asked Questions

How will I get support during the
program?
  • You'll receive support from your peers and a Senior Technical Mentor.
Will I receive a certificate upon
completion?
  • Yes, successful graduates of the program will receive a certificate.
Does the program provide job placement
assistance?
  • No we do not.
Where will the learning take place?
  • All learning resources and grading will be accessible through our Intranet platform.
Is there a monthly score threshold?
  • No, there is no monthly threshold but you are expected to complete your projects given on a weekly basis
Is the program online or onsite?
  • The program is fully virtual.
Can I defer participation in the program?
  • Unfortunately, deferrals are not available yet for this program.
What resources do I need to participate in
the program
  • A laptop and a commitment to dedicate a minimum of 20 hours weekly. Also, access to the internet and electricity.
What are the differences with the ALX SE
program
  • The ALX SE program is focused on fullstack software engineering while the introduction to software engineering is focused on introduction to web development.
What if I have a limited or no coding
background?
  • No worries! Our program is designed for individuals with zero coding background.
How intensive is the program?
  • While the program is designed to be manageable, it's also intensive enough to ensure you learn good
    foundational skills within four months.
Can I apply and participate in the program
if am currently an active learner in the
ALX SE, 70-hour-per-week program or
ALX program?
  • Yes, you can apply for and participate in the new Software Engineering Program even if you are currently
    an active learner in the intensive 70-hour-per-week
    program or any other ALX program. However, if selected, you would have to drop out from that program you are currently enrolled in.
